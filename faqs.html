LimitlessEnergy Frequently Asked Questions

1) Question:- What is Limitless Energy Logistics?
Answer:- Limitless Energy Logistics PVT. is a division of Limitless Incorporation that specializes in the transportation and supply of bulk fuel and energy-related products using road tankers.
2) Question:- Which countries do you send packages to?
Answer:- Operates across the SADC region (Southern Africa).
3) Question:- how do i order ?
Answer:- Get in touch with us through our website or visit our offices in Harare Zimbabwe. visit www.iata.org/en/publications/dgr/ or contact our customer services at www.limitlessenergylogistics.com/contact-us.
4) Question:- when can i order fuel in bulk ?
Answer:- Kindly check our website for updated information on the shipment dates on www.limitlessenergylogistics.com/announcements.
5) Question:- What is the minimum order quantity for bulk fuel ?
Answer: – The minimum order quantity for bulk fuel is 20,000 litres, however if you have a smaller quantity to ship please get in touch with our sales team and we will try to accommodate your needs.
6) Question:- How can I track my package ?
Answer:- Visit our homepage, www.limitlessenergylogistics.com, click on TRACK and enter your tracking ID given to you during booking.
7) Question:- Do you offer home deliveries
Answer:- Yes, we do offer home deliveries in Zimbabwe and the SADC region, however for bulk fuel deliveries we only do deliveries to business premises.
8) Question:- How much time does it take for my order to be delivered?
Answer: delivery time depends on the destination of the delivery and the current Covid-19 situation, however we will try to deliver your order on the same day we receive your booking for orders within Zimbabwe, for orders outside Zimbabwe delivery time is between 3 to 5 working days.
9) Question:- Can I pay in zimbabwean dollars?
Answer: - Yes, you can pay in Zimbabwean dollars, however for bulk fuel orders we only accept payment in US dollars.
10) Question:- How do I make a payment for bulk orders?
Answer: - you can make payment in Zimbabwean dollars or in US dollars, kindly get in touch with our sales team for more information on payment methods.
11) Question:- How do I raise a complaint?
Answer: - We are sorry to learn that you have cause to be disappointed with any aspect of our service as it is our commitment to deal with every query we receive in a professional and expedient manner. We regret any inconvenience caused by this incident. If you could kindly send an email to support@limitlesslogistics.com or visit www.limitlesslogistics.com/complaints and we will investigate your complaint so that the appropriate action can be taken.
12) Question:-Will you also do customs clearance on my behalf in Africa?
Answer:- You will be given two quotations during shipment booking , one which includes customs clearance in Africa & the other without customs clearance in Africa. Customs clearance in India will be done mandatorily by Limitless Logistics.
